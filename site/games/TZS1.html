TREEHOUSE ZOMBIE SURVIVOR

(Published Jun 12, 2022) Link to project on scratch

This is Treehouse Zombie Survivor Classic. This was the first installment in the TZS series. Am I particularly proud of it? Debatable. That said, this game, and many other games I made, are worth showing off so people know what I make and the evolution of my work.

INSTRUCTIONS

Survive for as long as you can against the zombies!!

use WASD to move, aim with mouse and hold mouse to do attacks
press 1 2 3 and 4 to choose through your inventory, press 5 to swap to weaponless / spec weapon
press I to drop items you dont need (moves other items up the inventory list)

click while holding an item to get it's respective SPEC

use keys Z X C and V to use SPEC moves!
(vampirism has no V move lol)

Collect items that give you SPECS

Kill zombies to buy from Dave in the treehouse (press SPACEBAR near the ladder)

SPECS- abilities that you can keep WITHOUT a weapon
WEAPON- says it in the name, can be used in conjunction with specs
