Teacher Wonder: Playable Specil was a joint project between me and my friend. Clearly, it was way out of the scope of scratch, however. I do think it's a fun thing to play no matter how short it turned out in the end.

INSTRUCTIONS

technically playable on scratch, but it's not recommended
PLAY ON TURBOWARP
https://turbowarp.org/1042869449
alt link: https://mirror.turbowarp.xyz/#1042869449
-------
Press Z to interact with interactable items/to progress through dialogue, hover over options with your mouse/use arrow keys to pick them
press X to dash (if Wonder is the one leading)(if leafeon is leading you get a shield)
Press Z to attack during fight segments when the enemy is open (they play an animation)
arrow keys to move to move
