BluGlu Battlegrounds 3


This is BluGlu Battlegrounds 3 (shortened to BGB3), which is the third installment of the BluGlu Battlegrounds series of fighting games I am making.
BGB3 is made using LÖVE, which is an amazing game development tool. It easily outclasses scratch in every regard.

BGB3 will contain many of my and my friends' OCs as playable fighters. That said, BGB3 will not release yet! Patience is key.

BGB3 is my first game made in Love2D that I'm wanting to share publically. More will be on the way!
