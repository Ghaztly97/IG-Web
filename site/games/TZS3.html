INSTRUCTIONS

ZXCV for moves. Mouse (holdable) for normal attack. and press SPACE for special move (sometimes phase 2 exclusive on some characters)
Q/E can be used on some characters

Double tap WASD to dash, hold afterwards to run
Hold F to block
Press 9 to open dev menu, press 8 to toggle zombie spawning, and press 0 to spawn sans zombie
